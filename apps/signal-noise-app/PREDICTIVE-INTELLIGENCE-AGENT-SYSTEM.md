## **Confidence Scoring Algorithm**

The system uses a multi-factor confidence scoring model:

### **Pattern Match Weight (35%)**
- **Strong Match**: 90-100% of pattern indicators present
- **Moderate Match**: 70-89% of pattern indicators present  
- **Weak Match**: 50-69% of pattern indicators present

### **Evidence Strength Weight (30%)**
- **Primary Evidence**: Direct organizational announcements
- **Secondary Evidence**: Industry reports, news articles
- **Supporting Evidence**: LinkedIn posts, partnership announcements

### **Historical Accuracy Weight (20%)**
- **Track Record**: Similar patterns in same organization type
- **Industry Trends**: Broader market movement validation
- **Seasonal Factors**: Time-of-year procurement patterns

### **Timeline Alignment Weight (15%)**
- **Cycle Due**: Organization in typical RFP issuance window
- **Budget Available**: Financial resources allocated
- **Staff Readiness**: Leadership team in place
