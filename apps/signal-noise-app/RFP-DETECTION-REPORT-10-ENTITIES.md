# 3-Phase LinkedIn RFP Detection Results - 10 Sports Entities

**Date:** November 10, 2025  
**Method:** 3-Phase LinkedIn Strategy using BrightData Search Engine  
**Entities Processed:** 10 South American Football Clubs

## Executive Summary

After conducting comprehensive 3-phase LinkedIn RFP detection across 10 major South American football clubs, **no active RFP opportunities** for digital/software/mobile app development were found. However, several **early digital transformation signals** were identified that indicate potential future opportunities.

## Methodology

### Phase 1: Direct RFP Searches
- Searched for explicit RFP/tender/licitación terms
- Keywords: RFP, tender, software development, mobile app, digital transformation
- 7 targeted searches across Peru and Ecuador clubs

### Phase 2: Digital Hiring Signals  
- LinkedIn Jobs monitoring for digital roles
- Keywords: Digital Project Manager, Software Development Manager, IT Manager
- 5 targeted searches on LinkedIn job postings

### Phase 3: Partnership Opportunities
- LinkedIn company posts and collaboration searches
- Keywords: partnership, collaboration, technology partnership, digital collaboration
- 4 targeted searches across major clubs
